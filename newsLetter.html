Industry Bite

It’s been a lively month for Australia’s smallest businesses. New ABN registrations are climbing again, led by trades, e-commerce and professional services, a sign that more people are backing themselves to work independently.

At the same time, the ATO is tightening its stance on compliance, sending early reminders on missed BAS and overdue GST. For many, it’s a nudge to bring their admin up to date before the year closes out.

Adding to the mix, new rules around super contributions and contractor classification are stirring questions about how “solo” work is defined. The next few months will test how flexible the system can be and how well independent operators can adapt to keep their businesses strong.
